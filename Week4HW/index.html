  • Bitcoin

    1. Bitcoin can be used as a savings vehicle. With large price fluctuations, Bitcoin may not seem to be a smart way to save money at first glance. But, since it's inception over 10 years ago, Bitcoin's price has increased 200% per year on average. With the top banks offering savings rates of .5% per year, it becomes clear why so many people have looked elsewhere to save money, and many people have landed on Bitcoin.
    2. Bitcoin is a hedge against inflation. It is called a "Hard Money", which means that it cannot be created by any central power. In the US, 40% of all dollars in circulation have been printed in the last year and a half. In the past, this type of increase in money supply has been a precursor to inflation. Becuase Bitcoin's supply is set by code, instead of political policy, it can be a great alternative for anyone wanting to store money in something besides paper currency that is prone to inflation.
